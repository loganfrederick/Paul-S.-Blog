Learning Through Error: A Geeky Lesson

Lying in bed made me find ways to pass time when I couldn’t sleep but was still in bed. One of those ways was to watch sports, which was usually one of either basketball (a sport I’m extremely familiar with) or soccer (a sport I enjoy but don’t know too much about).
